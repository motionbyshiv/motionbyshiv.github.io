Common questions

Before you ask.

Do you run the ads too?

No — media buying stays with you or your buyer, and that separation is deliberate: our only incentive is creative that performs. We speak fluent media-buyer, though: naming conventions, specs, and iteration requests all land the way ad ops expects.

How many variants do we get?

Depends on tier and mix — as a guide, an Animate subscription focused on ads typically produces 10 original concepts a month, each in 2–3 hook variants and every placement ratio. That’s a healthy testing cadence for most accounts spending under $100k/month.

What do you need from us to start?

Your offer, your current best ad (if any), brand assets, and access to performance data at whatever fidelity you’re comfortable sharing. First batch lands within the 7-day guarantee window.

Can you match competitors’ ad styles?

We can test into a format that’s working in your market — but as a variant lane, not the whole strategy. Copying alone caps you at second place.
