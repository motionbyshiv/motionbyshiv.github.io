Common questions

Before you ask.

What if our footage quality isn’t great?

Send it anyway. Audio cleanup, color correction, and smart cropping rescue more footage than people expect — and if something truly can’t be saved, we’ll tell you before spending your capacity on it.

Do you write hooks and captions, or just cut?

Both. Hook selection and caption copy are part of the edit — they’re usually the difference between 200 views and 20,000, so we don’t treat them as an afterthought.

Can you post directly to our channels?

We deliver publish-ready files with suggested copy; scheduling stays on your side so you keep account control. If you need posting handled end-to-end, ask about it on the call — it’s scoped case by case.

What does 20 edits a month actually mean?

Twenty finished assets — e.g. four long-form episodes each yielding five verticals. Simple format variants of the same edit count as one asset, not three.
