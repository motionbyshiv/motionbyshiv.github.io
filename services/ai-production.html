Stage gates

AI production is fast enough to be dangerous without discipline. Our stage gates keep the speed and remove the risk: you sign off on the concept before drafts, and on drafts before finals.

Common questions

The AI questions, answered straight.

Will it look AI-generated?

If it does, it doesn’t ship — that’s a hard rule, enforced by a senior human reviewing every frame. The generative layer produces volume; taste, brand fidelity, and final judgment are human, every time.

Is this safe for our brand? Who owns AI-produced assets?

You own 100% of delivered work, same as everything else we make. We work only with tools whose terms permit full commercial use, keep humans in the loop on anything resembling a real person or trademark, and will walk you through exactly what was used where — transparency is the policy.

What makes a good one-page brief?

Audience, the one thing they should believe after seeing the campaign, channels, and any hard constraints. That’s it — we’ll send you our brief template, and the discovery call fills any gaps.

Why not just use these AI tools ourselves?

You could — the tools are public. What you’d be hiring is the other layer: 8 years of creative direction deciding what to make, what’s on-brand, and what to throw away. The tools are the instrument; the studio is the musician.
