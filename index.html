Questions, answered

The four things everyone asks first.

Which tier should we start with?

If you already produce raw content and just need it finished, Polish. Most SaaS and web3 teams land on Animate — original creative is usually the real bottleneck. Generate fits when you want full campaigns from a one-page brief. Unsure? Message us and we’ll tell you honestly — including if the cheaper tier is the right one.

Can we pause or cancel?

Yes, anytime, with a single message. Pausing freezes your remaining days and keeps your board intact; cancelling stops the next billing cycle. No lock-in, no exit fees.

Who owns the work?

You do — fully. Every delivered asset, source file, and system we build is yours, including if you cancel. We keep nothing hostage.

Will the creative feel AI-generated?

No. AI runs our production layer — the volume, the speed, the versioning. Every frame is then directed, refined, and approved by senior human eyes before you ever see it. What you’re paying for is judgment, not just output.
