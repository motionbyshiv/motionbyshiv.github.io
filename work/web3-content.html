The situation

Deep tech, thin content, a launch quarter approaching.

A protocol team with genuinely novel infrastructure had one exhausted content person and a feed that went silent for weeks between announcements. The mechanism was hard to explain in text, competitors were louder, and a token launch quarter was approaching.

The engagement

Animate tier at daily cadence. Week one built the motion identity — type behavior, transitions, a visual language for the protocol’s core concepts — so every subsequent piece was recognizable and fast to produce. From week two, the pipeline ran: a content calendar agreed weekly, evergreen explainers produced ahead, and reactive pieces turned around inside 48 working hours. Scripts went to the team for technical accuracy only.

The result

Thirty consecutive days of publishing without the team writing a single script. 1.2M views across channels, a visible lift in quality perception versus template-driven competitors, and a content system the launch quarter could be built on.

Deliverables

  • Motion identity system (reusable kit)
  • 22 original short-form explainer pieces
  • Reactive announcement clips, 48wh turnaround
  • Weekly content calendar, maintained by us
