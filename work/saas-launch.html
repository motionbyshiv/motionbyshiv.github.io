The situation

A strong product, an invisible launch history.

A self-serve B2B SaaS was three weeks from its biggest release. Previous launches had shipped with a blog post and a screen recording; signups spiked for a day and flattened. The team had no in-house creative and no time to hire.

The engagement

Generate-tier scope, compressed to the launch window. Week one: positioning call, script, and styleframes — all approved on the shared board. Week two: hero film animation and explainer drafts, with daily visibility. Week three: finishing, sound, and the 12-variant paid matrix built from the film’s strongest moments.

The result

Launch day shipped with a hero film on the homepage, an explainer in onboarding, and a full testing matrix in the ad account. Launch creative CTR came in 40% above the previous release, and the variant matrix gave the growth team a month of iteration runway.

Deliverables

  • 60s hero launch film (16:9 + cutdowns)
  • 45s product explainer for onboarding
  • 12 paid ad variants across 3 hooks × 4 formats
  • Launch-day asset kit, formatted per channel
