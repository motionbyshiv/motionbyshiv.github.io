Before you write

Three things worth knowing.

No message is too early

“We might need something in Q3” is a fine reason to talk. Early conversations make better briefs — and there’s no obligation attached to saying hi.

We’ll tell you if we’re wrong for it

Live-action shoots, pure media buying, or a niche we can’t serve well — if it’s not us, we say so in the first reply and point you somewhere better.

NDAs welcome

Pre-launch and confidential work is a large part of the studio. Send your NDA, or ask for our standard mutual one before sharing anything sensitive.
