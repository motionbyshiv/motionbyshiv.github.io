Quality & the AI question

Will the creative feel AI-generated?

No. AI runs our production layer — the volume, the speed, the versioning. Every frame is then directed, refined, and approved by senior human eyes before you ever see it. What you’re paying for is judgment, not just output.

Why should we pay you instead of using AI tools ourselves?

The tools are public; the judgment isn’t. What you’re hiring is 8 years of creative direction deciding what to make, what’s on-brand, and what to throw away — plus a production system already engineered around those tools. Teams that try the DIY route usually come back with a folder of almosts.

Is AI-produced work safe for our brand legally?

We work only with tools whose terms permit full commercial use, keep humans in the loop on anything involving real people or trademarks, and hand over full ownership of delivered work. If your legal team has questions, we’ll walk them through exactly what was used where.

Who actually does the work?

Senior creative direction from our founder on every project, our in-house production system for volume, and specialist collaborators when a project calls for them. What never happens: your work handed to a junior while a senior name stays on the pitch deck.

Pricing & tiers

Which tier should we start with?

If you already produce raw content and just need it finished, Polish ($1,495). Most teams land on Animate ($2,995) — original creative is usually the real bottleneck. Generate ($4,995) fits when you want full campaigns from a one-page brief. Unsure? Message us and we’ll tell you honestly — including if the cheaper tier is the right one.

Are there hidden costs?

No. Your tier price is the price. The only extras are optional add-ons (rush lane, extra volume blocks, premium music licensing) — and every one is quoted in writing before any work starts.

What happens if we need more than our tier covers one month?

Add a volume block for that month only, or upgrade at the next cycle. We’ll flag it proactively if your request queue is outgrowing your tier — and equally if it’s undershooting it.

Do you offer one-off projects instead of subscriptions?

Occasionally, when the calendar allows. Message us with scope and timing. Subscribers always get priority, which is most of the reason the subscription exists.

Speed & process

How fast is delivery, exactly?

First drafts of standard assets land within 48 working hours of us starting them — and we tell you when that is. Larger pieces like brand films get a written timeline before we begin. First delivery is within 7 days of onboarding, guaranteed.

How does the 7-day guarantee work?

If your first deliverable isn’t in your hands within 7 days of onboarding, your first month is free. No conditions buried in footnotes — it exists to make trying us a zero-risk decision.

How many revisions do we get?

Two rounds per asset on Polish, three on Animate and Generate (per stage for campaigns). A round means consolidated feedback. Post-delivery fixes — typos, export issues — are always free and never count as revisions.

What do you need from us?

Three things: assets and brand files collected once at onboarding, one named feedback owner, and feedback within 2 working days to hold timelines. That’s the entire client-side job.

Do you work with clients outside India?

Yes — most of our pipeline is built for async collaboration across time zones. Briefs, drafts, and feedback live on a shared board; work moves while you sleep; calls happen in your hours.

Ownership & commitment

Who owns the work?

You do — fully. Every delivered asset, source file, and system we build is yours, including if you cancel. We keep nothing hostage.

Can we pause or cancel anytime?

Yes — one message does it. Pausing freezes your remaining days and keeps your board intact; cancelling stops the next billing cycle. No lock-in, no exit fees, and you leave with every file.

Will you sign an NDA?

Yes, happily — pre-launch work is a big part of what we do. Send yours or ask for our standard mutual NDA.

Can you show us work relevant to our industry?

Message us with your industry and we’ll share relevant samples privately — some of the best work stays off the public site for NDA or client-preference reasons.
