Our process

No mystery between your first message and your first delivery.

Most agencies keep their process vague because vagueness protects them. Ours is mapped to the day — because when you know exactly what happens next, saying yes stops feeling like a risk.

Phase 1 · Before you pay anything

Discovery — a conversation, not a pitch cycle.

  1. Step 1 · Your first message · Day 0

    You message us on WhatsApp

    Two sentences is enough: what you’re building and what you need creative for. No form, no scheduling link maze. We reply within one working day — usually within hours.

  2. Step 2 · Discovery call · Within 2 days

    A 30-minute discovery session

    One call, no prep needed on your side. We come out of it aligned on four things:

    • Goals — what the creative has to achieve (launch, pipeline, retention, awareness)
    • Channels — where it will live, which decides formats and cadence
    • Volume — how much you actually need monthly, mapped to a tier
    • Fit — and if we’re not the right studio for it, we’ll say so and point you somewhere better
  3. Step 3 · Written summary · Next working day

    Everything in writing

    You get a short written summary on WhatsApp: recommended tier, first-month deliverables, timeline, and price. No 40-page proposal — one message you can forward to a co-founder and decide on in five minutes.

Phase 2 · Week one

Onboarding — one focused day, then we’re producing.

  1. Step 4 · Kickoff & asset collection · Day 1

    We collect everything once

    A single structured intake so you never get piecemeal requests later:

    • Brand guidelines — logo files, fonts, colors, tone. No formal guide? We build a working mini-guide with you in under an hour, free
    • Assets — product shots, footage, screen access, past creative that worked (and didn’t)
    • Access — a shared drive both sides can reach, plus your review board
    • People — one feedback owner on your side, so approvals never stall in a committee
  2. Step 5 · First briefs · Day 1–2

    We write the first briefs — you approve them

    We turn the discovery call into concrete briefs for the first deliverables: reference, message, format, deadline. You approve or adjust each one in minutes on your board. Nothing gets produced against a brief you haven’t seen.

  3. Step 6 · First drafts · Within 48 working hours

    Production starts — and you can watch

    Senior creative direction over our AI production system. Drafts land on your shared board as they’re ready — you see progress, not a two-week silence followed by a “big reveal.”

  4. Step 7 · First delivery · By day 7, guaranteed

    Your first finished assets, in your channels’ formats

    Delivered ready to publish. If this doesn’t happen within 7 days of onboarding, your first month is free — that guarantee is on every tier, in writing.

Phase 3 · Every week after

The running engine — feedback, revisions, delivery, repeat.

Feedback & approvals

Comment where the work lives

Every draft sits on your shared board with frame-accurate commenting. You react there or just voice-note us on WhatsApp — we translate it into changes. One feedback owner on your side keeps every cycle to days, not weeks. We ask for feedback within 2 working days to hold timelines; if you need longer, timelines shift transparently, never silently.

Revisions

Included rounds, honest edges

Every tier includes revision rounds per asset (2 on Polish, 3 on Animate and Generate). A round means consolidated feedback, not one comment at a time. Nearly everything lands inside the included rounds — and if feedback keeps moving past them, we flag it early and agree next steps in writing before continuing.

Communication

One channel, no account managers

You talk to the people making the work, on WhatsApp, in one thread. Weekly you get a short status note: what shipped, what’s in progress, what we need from you. No meetings that should have been messages.

Delivery & handover

Formatted, named, and yours

Finals arrive in every agreed format, sensibly named, in your shared drive. Source files are handed over on request at any time — you own 100% of everything we make, permanently, even if you cancel tomorrow.

Phase 4 · The long game

Post-delivery support and the ongoing relationship.

A subscription isn’t a stack of transactions — it’s a creative team that compounds. Every month we know your brand better, your briefs get shorter, and the work gets sharper.

  • Monthly creative review (Animate and up): what performed, what we learned, what we’ll try next month
  • Post-delivery fixes: spot a typo or export issue after delivery? Fixed free, any time — that’s quality, not a revision
  • Performance feedback loop: share your numbers and we iterate creative against them — winners get variants, losers get autopsies
  • Pause without penalty: seasonal business? Freeze your days, keep your board, resume when it picks up
  • Clean exits: if you leave, you leave with everything — files, sources, and a handover note your next team can actually use

Process questions

Asked before, answered here.

How fast is “fast,” exactly?

First drafts of standard assets land within 48 working hours of us starting them — and we tell you when that is. Larger pieces like brand films get a written timeline before we begin. First delivery is within 7 days of onboarding, guaranteed.

What do you need from us to keep timelines?

Three things: assets and access collected once at onboarding, one named feedback owner, and feedback within 2 working days. That’s the whole client-side job description.

What tools do we need to learn?

None. WhatsApp for conversation, a link to your review board for feedback, a shared drive for files. If you can comment on a video and send a message, you can run this engine.

What if we don’t like the first drafts?

That’s what the approved-brief step and revision rounds are for — drafts move toward your taste fast because feedback happens on the actual work. And if the fit truly isn’t there in week one, the 7-day guarantee means you haven’t risked the month.

Do you work across time zones?

Yes — asynchronously by default. Briefs, drafts, and feedback all live on the board, so work moves while you sleep. Calls get scheduled inside your working hours.
