Ecommerce-specific questions

Asked by brands like yours.

Our product photos are basic. Is that enough to work from?

Usually, yes. Clean packshots on white are a perfectly good starting point — the motion, environment, and styling happen in production. Send a few over WhatsApp and we’ll tell you straight what they can become.

Will AI-generated scenes misrepresent the product?

The product itself stays photographically true — that’s a hard rule, for your returns rate as much as our ethics. Generative techniques build the world around the product; they don’t redraw what you’re selling.

Can you handle our volume in Q4?

Yes, if we plan it — subscribers can add extra volume blocks for peak months, and we start seasonal production in advance. Q4 capacity is finite, which is one more reason the subscription model beats scrambling for a vendor in October.
